Spider Powers

Bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker gained extraordinary abilities that make him one of the most formidable heroes on Earth.

🕸️

Web Slinging

Fires strong adhesive web fluid from mechanical web-shooters. Can swing across entire city skylines at incredible speed.

⚡

Super Strength

Can lift up to 10 tons with ease — enough to stop a moving train or hold up an entire collapsing building.

🧠

Spider-Sense

Precognitive sixth sense that warns of imminent danger before it can be perceived by normal senses.

🦎

Wall-Crawling

Can cling to virtually any surface using microscopic hairs on hands and feet. No surface is too smooth.

💨

Super Speed

Reflexes and speed far beyond any Olympic athlete — can dodge bullets and react in milliseconds.

🧬

Healing Factor

Accelerated healing lets him recover from injuries that would permanently sideline any ordinary person.

Origin Timeline

The journey from a nerdy Queens teenager to Earth's greatest web-slinging protector.

1962 / Amazing Fantasy #15

The Spider Bite

Peter Parker, a shy 15-year-old science whiz, is bitten by a radioactive spider at a science exhibit. His life changes forever.

First Weeks

Great Responsibility

After refusing to stop a burglar, Peter watches Uncle Ben die by that same criminal. The defining lesson of his life.

Year One

The Daily Bugle

Peter lands a job at the Daily Bugle, secretly selling photos of himself as Spider-Man to J. Jonah Jameson.

Year Three

The Sinister Six

Six of Spider-Man's greatest enemies unite to destroy him. He faces them entirely alone — and prevails.

Year Five

The Black Suit

An alien symbiote attaches to Peter, enhancing his powers while slowly influencing his personality toward aggression.

Today

The Multiverse Era

Across every reality, every timeline — Spider-Man endures. The symbol of hope and perseverance lives on.
